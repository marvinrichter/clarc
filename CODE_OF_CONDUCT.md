# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

clarc is a technical project. We expect participants to:

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people
- Accept differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Help others learn and contribute effectively

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting

Report issues to **marvin@marvinrichter.dev**. All reports are reviewed confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, and contributions that violate this code, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for repeated violations.
